The Helios Ensemble is a community of volunteers who perform advanced choral repertoire at a professional level. Come to our concert, "Music for Kings and Queens," featuring Handel, Purcell, Parry, Howells, and more!
Enjoy TSO orchestra players, an Aeoling-Skinner Organ, and the Helios Ensemble on Sunday, March 22nd, 3:30 P.M. at Christ the King Episcopal, 2800 West Ina Road, Tucson, AZ 8574110. Dollar donation suggested.
